Market Need

The problem we are solving is of exponentially increasing urban pollution along others threatening phenomena like climate change and Global warming. You can estimate the magnitude of the problem by the following facts:

  • More than 90 per cent of the world population is exposed to unhealthy air.
  • Air pollution contributes to 9% (Nearly 5 Million) of deaths globally. ( While reading it like this, it feels just like a number but if you think about it, you'll realize how frightening it would be to lose a loved one because of pollution.)
  • The world loses more than 8,000 square meters of tree cover per second.
  • Earth's temperature has risen by 0.14° F (0.08° C) per decade since 1880, and the rate of warming over the past 40 years is more than twice that: 0.32° F (0.18° C) per decade since 1981 ( 0.08degrees Celsius) per decade since 1880.
  • Since 1870, global sea levels have risen by about 8 inches.

What we Intend to Do


We intend to solve this problem by inculcating vertical forestry. It's a highly efficient technique introduced by Stefano Boeri, which we've optimized for Indian conditions by handpicking the perfect plants suitable for domestic environmental conditions. We'll be bringing aesthetics of your favorite forest, right into your home and fresh air to breathe in, by planting trees, herbs, and shrubs on the roof, walls, and balconies. Massive reforestation isn’t a pipe dream and it can have real benefits for people. Apart from the external pollution To make the maintenance and the overall experience of forestry easier, we are also working on creating an IoT-based irrigation system that will use Arduino (an electronic prototyping platform) and greywater. We also plan to implement our custom soil engineering to increase fertility as well as reduce the weight of the soil. After we successfully implement vertical forestry, we also plan to include other emerging concepts like solar energy, organic farming, etc.









What Makes Us Unique

  • Excessive water requirement: We will introduce our water management system by introducing grey water for plants irrigation

  • Insects and Mosquitos: Our specific plants (E.g. Lemon Balm, Basil, Cymbopogon Citratus) would also repel mosquitos and unwanted insects.
